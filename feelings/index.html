The MyDopa Feeling

You already know the feeling. Here's what it actually feels like.

MyDopa is the feeling right before proof arrives. You already know — and the knowing is the best part.

← What does MyDopa mean?

You already know

The Coffee

Morning. Both hands around the cup. You haven't tasted it yet. But you can smell it. Feel the warmth through the cup. You already know. This first sip is going to be so good. That's MyDopa. That's anticipation, before the proof.

The Bread

Oven's still going. The whole kitchen already smells like it. It's not even done yet. But you already know. That first slice is going to be perfect. That's MyDopa. That's satisfaction, before the taste.

The Shot

Par three. You pull the seven iron. One practice swing — feel it, breathe, settle in. Then you hit it. Contact. You know before you even look up. It's tracking straight at the pin. It hasn't landed yet. But you already know. That's going in. That's MyDopa. That's certainty, before it lands.

The Sunset

Beach. Kids next to you. Sun dropping toward the water. Everyone goes quiet. It's coming. That last flash of gold. That's MyDopa. That's awe, before it's gone.

The Birth

Standing next to your wife. Any second now. You haven't seen his face yet. But your heart already knows him. That's MyDopa. That's love, before you've even met.

The Wave

Paddling. The swell lifts you from behind. You haven't stood up yet. But you already know. This one's good. That's MyDopa. That's thrill, before you drop in.

The Launch

Months of nights on this. Nobody's seen it yet. You hit publish. The inbox is silent — no orders, no replies, nothing. But you lean back, and you already know it's real. You didn't quit. That's MyDopa. That's pride, before anyone claps.

The Summit

Legs burning on the last incline. The trees break open ahead. You haven't stepped out to see the view yet. But the air changes. And you already know what's waiting. That's MyDopa. That's triumph, before the view.

The Reply

You sent it twenty minutes ago. Then you saw it — the three dots. They haven't sent anything yet. But you're already smiling at your phone. That's MyDopa. That's hope, before the words.

The Lift

Gym floor. Bar loaded heavier than you've ever pulled. Chalk on your hands. You grip it, set your back, brace. You haven't pulled it off the ground yet. But your body already knows. This weight is yours today. That's MyDopa. That's power, before the lift.

The Note

Mic in hand. The big note is coming up in the song. You feel it building in your chest before you open your mouth. You haven't sung it yet. But you already know. It's going to be perfect. That's MyDopa. That's command, before the sound.

The Proposal

Ring in your pocket. One knee about to hit the ground. You haven't asked yet. But you already know what she's going to say. That's MyDopa. That's joy, before yes.

The Pattern

Notice the pattern. Nothing's happened yet. But you already feel it. Human beings live for that feeling. Now it has a name.

Everybody feels it a little differently. How do you feel yours?
