The problem

Your brain is built to
protect you.
Not celebrate you.

One bad moment erases five good ones. Every day. Without your permission. This is the negativity bias — 40 years of neuroscience confirm it.

Your brain is not broken. It is doing exactly what evolution designed it to do. The problem is that no one ever showed you how to override the filter.
