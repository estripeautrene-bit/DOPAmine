Cookie Notice

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Overview

This Cookie Notice explains how MyDopa uses cookies and similar technologies on mydopa.app. Similar technologies may include local storage, scripts, tags, pixels, and device identifiers.

Essential Technologies

Essential technologies may operate automatically when they are required to deliver the website, maintain security, remember privacy choices, or submit the quiz and waitlist forms. These technologies are not used for advertising.

Analytics Technologies

MyDopa uses Google Analytics and Mixpanel to measure website visits, quiz progress, quiz completion, waitlist conversion, referral sources, and general interaction patterns.

Where prior consent is required, Google Analytics and Mixpanel remain disabled until you select "Accept Analytics." Selecting "Reject Analytics" does not prevent you from using the website, completing the quiz, receiving your result, or joining the waitlist.

Analytics information is retained for up to 14 months.

Cookie Banner

The banner will offer three choices with equally clear presentation:

  • Accept Analytics
  • Reject Analytics
  • Cookie Settings

You may change your choice later through the "Cookie Settings" link in the website footer. Withdrawing consent does not affect analytics processing that occurred before withdrawal.

Browser Controls

You may also control cookies and similar technologies through your browser. Blocking essential technologies may affect website functions. Browser settings may not replace the consent controls offered by the website.

Advertising

MyDopa does not currently use advertising cookies, advertising pixels, cross-context behavioral advertising, or targeted-advertising technologies.
