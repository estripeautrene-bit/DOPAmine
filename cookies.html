1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit — for example, whether you have dismissed a banner, or whether you are signed in.

3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

All cookies listed above are strictly necessary for the site and app to function. You cannot opt out of these without preventing the service from working correctly. They are set only on mydopa.app and its subdomains.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Common instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Deleting cookies will sign you out of your MyDopa account and reset your banner preferences.
